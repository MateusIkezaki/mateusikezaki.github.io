Who am I?

My name is Mateus Mori Ikezak(born December 13th, 2002 in São Paulo, Brazil) and this is my portfolio. I'm a passionate programmer and a game development enthusiast. I've started coding back when I was 12, in an iD Tech game development summer camp for beginners in New York City. I've never stopped ever since and after that camp I knew coding was what I wanted to persue. Once I got back to Brazil I started taking programming lessons in a computer science school called SuperGeeks in 2016. I love developing games during my spare time and just learning and studying more about computer science in general. In 2018 I decided to give web development a shot and fell in love with it immediately. Although I do have some knowledge in other fields in computer science, do keep in mind that my specialty is game development, in Unity Engine to be more precise.

Specialties

Unity Engine

Game Development on Unity is what I do best. I've been using it since 2017 and have published many projects in my itch.io page, which you can find in the 'Projects' page
