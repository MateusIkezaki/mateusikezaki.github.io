About Me

Hi there! My name is Mateus Mori Ikezaki, and I am currently a sophomore studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at UC Berkeley. As a huge fan of video games, my love for computer science began with game development, when I was 12 years old. Since then, I have fallen in love with game development, and have been making games in my spare time. I have 5 years of experience with Unity Engine and C#. I am always seeking to learn new skills and I truly believe in self-sufficiency, which is why I learned to make 2D animations and digital illustrations to make my own assets for my games. I also love programming websites and web applications with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Node.js, in fact the website you are currently in was programmed by yours truly, with no external libraries. I have taken college courses on data and machine structures, and am proficient in Java, Python, C, and RISC-V as well.
